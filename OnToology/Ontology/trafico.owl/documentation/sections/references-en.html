References back to ToC

The vocabulary of urban motor vehicle traffic has basically three parts: Traffic Equipment, Sections and Incidents.

For both three, the following vocabularies have been reused: WGS84, Geocore and Geosparql for the geographic representation of the three entities.

For the different types of traffic devices estraf:EquipoTrafico and for the types of incidence estraf:Incidencia the taxonomy vocabularies Skos has been reused.

The estraf:DispositivoMedicionTrafico devices, as it is a matter of subclass of sosa:Sensor too, Sosa vocabulary has been broadly reused..

Moreover, for the representation of time intervals of estraf:ObservacionTrafico, Time vocabulary has been reused.

Finally, in the representation of estraf:Tramo the escjr vocabulary has been reused.
