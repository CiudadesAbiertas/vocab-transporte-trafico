This version:
http://vocab.ciudadesabiertas.es/def/transporte/trafico/1.0.0
Revision:
This is the first draft of the ontology of Traffic that has been developed in the framework of the project Plataforma de gobierno abierto, colaborativa e interoperable in which participate the city councils of Madrid, Zaragoza, Santiago de Compostela and A Coruña.
